# 60-Second Pitch — Partypop
## Your Kid's Birthday, Planned in 60 Seconds

---

### 🪝 Hook (5 sec)

"Thirty million kids' birthday parties happen in the US every year. Every single one starts the same way: a parent on Pinterest at midnight, overwhelmed before the first balloon is blown up. Partypop ends that."

---

### 😰 Problem (10 sec)

"Planning a kid's birthday party takes weeks. Pinterest gives you ideas but no plan. Your shopping list lives across three apps. RSVPs arrive through text, email, and 'oh I forgot to mention at pickup.' You spend three hundred and fourteen dollars on average — and the planning is more stressful than the party itself. The bounce house company you found on Google has great reviews but showed up two hours late to three parties in your neighborhood. You had no way to know."

---

### 💡 Solution (15 sec)

"Partypop takes four inputs — theme, age, headcount, budget — and generates a complete party plan in sixty seconds. A minute-by-minute day-of timeline. Age-appropriate activities with instructions. A food and drink menu. A decoration checklist. A shopping list with quantities calculated for your headcount. Ten themed templates — dinosaurs, princess, superhero, Minecraft — each with curated activities that actually work for the right age group. And the feature that makes parents share it: built-in RSVP tracking. Share one link. Guests tap to confirm. Dietary restrictions, plus-ones, and arrival times all in one place. No group text. No spreadsheet. No forgotten invite."
