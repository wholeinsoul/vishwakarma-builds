# 60-Second Investor Pitch — On Special
## AI Specials-to-Content Tool for Bars & Restaurants
**Date:** 2026-03-17

---

## Hook (5 sec)

"On Special turns a bar's daily specials into ready-to-post social media content in 30 seconds — so bar owners can stop worrying about Instagram and get back to pouring drinks."

---

## Problem (10 sec)

"Today, 700,000+ restaurant and bar owners in the U.S. struggle with a simple but costly problem: they can't post their daily specials consistently. They're behind the bar during peak hours when posting matters most. The result? Empty seats while the bar down the street — the one with the active Instagram — is packed. Restaurants that invest in social media see a 9.9% revenue increase (Deloitte, 2025). The ones that don't are leaving $50,000-$150,000 per year on the table. The alternative — hiring a social media manager — costs $500-$2,000/month. That's overkill for 'post tonight's specials.'"

---

## Solution (15 sec)

"On Special is a specials-to-content engine built specifically for bars and restaurants. The owner types tonight's specials — '$5 margaritas, half-price wings, DJ at 10' — and in 30 seconds gets platform-ready posts for Instagram (with hashtags), Facebook, and Google Business. Each post is tuned to the bar's brand voice — casual dive bar, upscale cocktail lounge, neighborhood pub. Template modes for common events: happy hour, live music night, sports game, themed party. No Canva. No ChatGPT prompting. No 15-minute content creation workflow. Type. Generate. Post. Done."

---

## Traction (10 sec)

"Pre-launch, but the signals are strong. 'Restaurant happy hours near me' gets 18,100 monthly searches — up 83% year-over-year — with LOW competition. Comparable vertical SaaS tools in hospitality (MustHaveMenus, FoodShot AI) are growing in a $196B social media software market (Verified Market Research, projected 2032 at 14.84% CAGR). Bar owners on Reddit, Facebook groups, and hospitality forums consistently cite social media management as their #1 non-operations pain point. We're validating with a 10-bar cohort in a single neighborhood before scaling."

---

## Market (5 sec)

"The U.S. has 700,000+ restaurants and 60,000+ bars. Our SAM — bars and casual dining venues running daily specials — is 240,000 venues at $99/month, a $285M annual opportunity. The broader restaurant social media tools market is growing at 15% annually."

---

## Team (5 sec)

"Solo technical founder with full-stack AI product experience. Built and shipped overnight MVPs across SaaS verticals. Deep understanding of B2B SMB dynamics and hospitality pain points from direct research and validation."

---

## Ask (10 sec)

"We're bootstrapping to first revenue. Initial investment: $0 infrastructure (AI APIs are usage-based, hosting is commodity). The milestone: 100 paying bars in 3 cities within 6 months, generating $120K ARR. That proves the unit economics and unlocks Phase 2 — POS integration that connects posting activity to same-night sales data. That closed loop turns a content tool into an intelligence platform, and that's where the real defensible value lives."

---

## One-Liner

> Rain at 4. Half-price wings at 5. A packed room by 7. That used to be instinct. On Special makes it data.
