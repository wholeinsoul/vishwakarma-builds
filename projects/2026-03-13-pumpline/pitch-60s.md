# 60-Second Pitch — Pumpline
## The Trusted Septic Provider Directory

---

### 🪝 Hook (5 sec)

"Pumpline is solving the trust crisis in septic services for 33 million rural American homeowners who have no way to find a reliable provider."

---

### 😰 Problem (10 sec)

"Today, when a septic system fails — and that's raw sewage in your yard — rural homeowners have zero trust infrastructure. No verified reviews, no pricing transparency, no service records. They call whoever their neighbor's cousin recommends and hope for the best. The result: $3,000 surprise fees, no-show contractors, and a $15,000-to-$30,000 replacement with no second opinion. Every other home service has Angi, Yelp, Thumbtack. Septic has nothing."

---

### 💡 Solution (15 sec)

"We've built an SEO-optimized, county-by-county septic provider directory with what the big platforms refuse to offer: septic-specific trust signals. Verified work history, transparent pricing ranges, response times, photos of completed jobs, and reviews from actual neighbors — not anonymous internet strangers. Providers get a premium profile that generates qualified local leads. Homeowners search for free and find the 3–5 best providers in their county ranked by what actually matters. We're not trying to be Angi for everything. We own the septic vertical, county by county."

---

### 📈 Traction (10 sec)

"The search demand is already there and nobody's capturing it. 'Septic tank pumping services' gets 110,000 monthly searches with LOW keyword competition — an 8,338% growth trend. Existing niche directories like septiccompaniesnearme.com have 3,800+ listings but zero community trust signals and thin content. Facebook Groups with 150K+ members and Reddit communities with 2.5M+ members are where people actually go for recommendations today — proving the demand for trusted provider info is massive and completely unstructured."

---

### 🌍 Market (5 sec)

"The U.S. septic services industry is $11.2 billion, growing 4–8% annually. 33 million homes rely on septic systems. The online lead-gen slice for home services is a $22 billion market — and septic's share is wide open."

---

### 👤 Team (5 sec)

"We're a technical founder with AI-assisted development capability, launching lean with zero overhead. We don't need a team of 20 — we need 3 counties, 15 providers, and 6 months of SEO compounding."

---

### 💰 Ask (10 sec)

"This is a bootstrapped build. Initial investment: under $1,000 for hosting, domain, and initial provider outreach. Month 6 target: 15 paying providers at $75–$100/month = $1,125 MRR. Month 12 target: 40 providers, $4,000 MRR. The first milestone is proving provider conversion: can we turn free-trial listings into $50–$200/month subscriptions at a 25%+ rate? If yes, we have a repeatable county-by-county expansion playbook that scales to $150K+ ARR within 24 months."

---

*Total runtime: ~60 seconds at natural speaking pace.*
