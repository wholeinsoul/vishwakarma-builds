# IdeaBrowser Idea of the Day — March 26, 2026

## Title
**Learn-to-Draw App with AI Feedback on Every Sketch** (Markday)

## URL Slug
`daily-drawing-fundamentals-trainer`

## Problem Description
Art education is built for people who are already drawing. Long courses assume commitment. YouTube tutorials assume context. The hobbyist who wants to improve but has twenty minutes after the kids are in bed has never had a system built for them. That person is everywhere the system isn't.

## Solution
Markday is a daily drawing trainer that builds skill through short, structured lessons. Each one takes under ten minutes and connects directly to the last. Gesture work builds into shading. Shading builds into form. AI feedback on uploaded sketches catches the specific mistakes a tutorial never pauses to address: a proportion issue in the left shoulder, a shading gradient that flattens instead of rounds. The habit is the product. Ten minutes, three sessions a week, and every uploaded sketch feeds a model that learns where beginners break down.

## MVP Strategy
Start with twenty core lessons covering the fundamentals: line control, gesture, basic shading, simple composition. Build a sketch upload tool that returns AI feedback on proportion and line confidence. Onboard thirty hobbyists from r/learntoart and watch where they stop coming back. Dropout points are the product roadmap. The metric is whether users complete a third lesson within their first week.

## Validation Metric
Whether users complete a third lesson within their first week. That threshold separates habit-builders from one-time visitors.

## Revenue Model
- $5/month or $50/year (annual plan is real conversion target)
- Community colleges / continuing education = institutional buyer
- License AI feedback layer at per-student rates
- Sketch data → curriculum intelligence for administrators
